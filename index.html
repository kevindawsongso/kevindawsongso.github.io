About Me

Hey—I’m Kevin Dawson, a tech professional based in Greensboro, NC with a background that bridges IT systems, endpoint security, and cybersecurity. I started out in enterprise IT support and gradually shifted toward security, where I found purpose in threat detection, incident response, and building smarter, more resilient systems.

I’m at my best in hands-on environments. Whether I’m deploying honeypots on AWS, fine-tuning SIEM alerts in Splunk, or running lab simulations to validate controls, I stay focused on practical, real-world application. I don’t just want to run the tools—I want to understand how they work, why they matter, and how to use them to solve real problems.

Outside the terminal, I value structure, clarity, and helping others grow. I’m the one who documents the process, asks the “why” behind the task, and shares what I’ve learned. I’ve worked with nonprofits and enterprise teams alike to align technology with mission-critical outcomes.

This site is where I share my projects, labs, and lessons as I level up in the field. If you're here to connect, collaborate, or just talk shop—you’re in the right place.
