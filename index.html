About Me

I’m Kevin Dawson, a tech professional based in Greensboro, NC. My background bridges IT systems, endpoint security, and a dedicated focus on cybersecurity. I began my journey in enterprise IT support, which provided a strong foundation in understanding user needs and system intricacies. This experience naturally led me toward security, where I discovered a passion for the proactive and analytical challenges of threat detection, incident response, and engineering resilient systems.

I thrive in hands-on environments. Whether it’s deploying honeypots on AWS, fine-tuning SIEM alerts in Splunk, simulating attacks in a lab to validate security controls, or scripting solutions in Python, my focus is always on practical, real-world application. I believe in not just using the tools, but deeply understanding how they work, why they are important, and how they can be leveraged to solve tangible security problems.

Beyond the command line, I value structure, clear communication, and fostering growth in others. I'm often the one documenting processes, questioning the "why" behind a task to ensure alignment with broader goals, and sharing knowledge with my team. My experience includes working with both nonprofit organizations and enterprise teams, always aiming to align technology effectively with mission-critical outcomes. This site serves as a platform to share my projects, lab work, and the lessons I learn as I continue to advance in the cybersecurity field.
